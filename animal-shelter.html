Exotic Animals

Alcoholic Barney

Barney is a large purple dinosaur. During the 90's he was joyful, fun and loving. After Y2K he had a spiral downhill and looked for answers at the bottom of a liquor bottle. He never found them but he found us. He is a new dinosaur and would love to find a new home!

Perry the Platypus

Perry came to us from all the way down under! For those of you who may not know he is a duck-billed, beaver-tailed, otter-footed mammal. He is venomous but if you stay on his good side, he's got your back!

Charmander

Charmander will live forever! He can evolve, but never dies. You will need to include him in your will. Due to his eternal flame on his tail, we highly recommend a smoker adopts him! The money saved on lost lighters will result in a positive investment.
